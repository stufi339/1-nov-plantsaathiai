## Common Questions (‡§∏‡§æ‡§Æ‡§æ‡§®‡•ç‡§Ø ‡§™‡•ç‡§∞‡§∂‡•ç‡§®)

### Q: How often are prices updated?
**A**: Daily. Government updates prices every day from mandis across India.

### Q: Are these real prices?
**A**: Yes! These are official wholesale prices from AGMARKNET Portal (Government of India).

### Q: Can I sell at these prices?
**A**: These are wholesale prices. Your actual price may vary slightly based on quality, quantity, and negotiation.

### Q: What if my crop is not listed?
**A**: Try searching with different names or check "All Commodities" list. Some crops may be listed under different varieties.

### Q: Why no prices for today?
**A**: Prices are updated after mandi closes. Check previous day's prices or refresh in the evening.
