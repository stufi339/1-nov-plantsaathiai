## â“ FAQ

**Q: Why can't I update data immediately?**
A: Satellite data doesn't change minute-by-minute. Daily updates provide the best balance of freshness and performance.

**Q: What if I need data right now?**
A: If 24 hours have passed, click "Update Satellite Data". Otherwise, the cached data is still accurate.

**Q: How do I know if my data is current?**
A: Look for the green "Data is up-to-date" indicator with time remaining.

**Q: What happens if I'm offline?**
A: Cached data will still load, but you won't be able to fetch fresh data until you're back online.

**Q: Can I change the 24-hour period?**
A: Currently, the 24-hour period is fixed for optimal performance and accuracy.
