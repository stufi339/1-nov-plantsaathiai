## üé® Design Philosophy

### Color Psychology
- **Blue** (Weather) - Trust, reliability, water
- **Green** (Yield) - Growth, success, nature
- **Red/Orange** (Alerts) - Urgency, attention, action
- **Purple/Pink** (Marketplace) - Premium, quality
- **White/Blur** (Education) - Clean, focus, learning

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Critical Info** (Top) - Weather, alerts, tasks
2. **Quick Actions** (Middle) - Easy access to tools
3. **Educational** (Middle-Bottom) - Learning content
4. **Recommendations** (Bottom) - Shopping, extras

### Farmer-First Design
- **Large touch targets** - Easy to tap on mobile
- **Visual icons** - Universal understanding
- **Minimal text** - Quick comprehension
- **Rich media** - Videos and images engage better
- **Local language** - Comfortable communication
